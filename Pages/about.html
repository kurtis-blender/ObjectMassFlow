ObjectMassFlow.net

I am Arthur Rowe Jones, usually found online under the name Kurtis Blender. As previously mentioned, I am a maker, writer, and mender.
I have built this website to collect and display things I create. But also, perhaps, to assemble, piece by piece, a project with a larger scope...

I hope it to be:
An archive. A sensemaking project. A route to interacting with like-minded others.
A collection of works that intertwine, juxtaposing and connecting their subject matters, mediums, and participants.
With the hope of forming widely rooted meshes of meanings.
Perhaps most of all, to house the perpetual work-in-progress that is trying to interpret pieces of the world into insight and resonance.

Topics may include: infrastructure, ecologies, the climate crisis, consumerism, finance, supply chains, manafacturing, craft, retail, desire, function, objects, materialism, cultures, history, futures, socialism, care, design, repair, wood, stories, content, internet, semiotics, networks, systems, assemblages, growth

Mediums may include: writing, collage, filmmaking, woodwork, sewing, drawing, photography, printmaking, audio
Mending merits mention, but does not fit neatly as a 'medium', but I plan to give any and all repairs their due.

Notes on the name:
ObjectMassFlow.net has a multiple inferences I find evocative, but its primary explanation can be eluded to by three definitions:
Object: a material thing that can be seen and touched.1
Root Mass Flow: the process by which nutrients move to the roots via water.2
Mass Flow Rate: the mass of a substance which passes per unit of time.3
